Hoperiver Residence is a family home located in Chilliwack, BC. The project was completed in 2020. The design concept was to create a modern, yet cozy space for the family to enjoy. The design was inspired by the client's love for the outdoors and the desire to create a space that would bring the outdoors in. The design was completed in collaboration with the client and the builder. The project was completed in 2020.
