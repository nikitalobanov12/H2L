About

H2L Interior Design Studio, a boutique interior design firm based in Richmond, BC, specializes in residential and commercial interior design. Offering a wide range of services, the studio helps clients achieve their dream spaces.

Passionate and creative designers at H2L are dedicated to providing clients with top-notch service. Committed to creating beautiful and functional spaces, H2L ensures each design reflects the unique personalities and lifestyles of its occupants.

The belief that every space should mirror the people who live in it drives H2L to craft spaces that are both functional and beautiful, enduring the test of time.

Mission

At H2L Interior Design Studio, the mission is to create exceptional and memorable spaces that elevate the lives of those who experience them. Believing that great design has the power to inspire, engage, and transform environments, H2L fosters a positive impact on the well-being and productivity of its occupants.

Recognizing the importance of preserving the planet for future generations, H2L incorporates sustainable materials and practices into designs whenever possible. Embracing innovation, H2L stays up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies to deliver cutting-edge design solutions that are environmentally responsible and visually stunning.

Vision

The vision for H2L Interior Design Studio is to shape the future of interior design by continually pushing the boundaries of creativity, functionality, and sustainability. Striving to be a catalyst for positive change, H2L aspires to create spaces that seamlessly blend form and function, while respecting the environment and promoting well-being.

Championing eco-conscious design principles and practices that reduce the environmental footprint of each project. By integrating innovative materials and energy-efficient solutions, H2L seeks to set new standards in sustainable design, making a lasting and positive impact on the planet.

We believe that every space should be a reflection of the people who live in it. We strive to create spaces that are both functional and beautiful, and that will stand the test of time.
