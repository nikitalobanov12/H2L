About

H2L Interior Design Studio is a boutique interior design firm based in Richmond, BC. We specialize in residential and commercial interior design, and offer a wide range of services to help you achieve your dream space.

We are a team of passionate and creative designers who are dedicated to providing our clients with the best possible service. We are committed to creating beautiful and functional spaces that reflect our clients' unique personalities and lifestyles.

We believe that every space should be a reflection of the people who live in it. We strive to create spaces that are both functional and beautiful, and that will stand the test of time.
