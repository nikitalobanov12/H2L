Madeira Cabin

Details

Location: Sunshine Coast, BC
Year: 2021
Sector: Residential
Designer: Louis Le

Project Description:
Located in the Madeira Park area of Sunshine Coast, BC. This cabin is a weekend getaway for the owners and their family. The cabin is a 2 bedroom, 1 bathroom, 1 kitchen, and 1 living room space. with 800 sqft built on a 1 acre lot, surrounded by trees and is a short walk to the beach. This cabin is designed to be a cozy and comfortable space for the owners and their family to enjoy.
